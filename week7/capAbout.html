What is Starline Bookkeeping?

Starline Bookkeeping is a sole proprietorship created to assist small businesses with a multitude of bookkeeping services. These services include daily upkeep, weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly financial reports, quarterly or yearly tax readiness assistance, and more. Starline Bookkeeping was designed to help grow small businesses by managing cash-flow and providing feedback for improving income organization.

Starline's Inspiration

Starline's manager, Amber Freeman, was inspired to create Starline Bookkeeping for two reasons:
  • Personal experience of the hardships in trying to keep up with finances alongside running a successful business
  • Motivation to give back services to small local businesses

Ms. Freeman is the daughter of micro-business owner Curtis Freeman of Freeman's Automotive and Armory LLC, and personally witnessed the challenges and stress her father faced by managing all odds and ends on his own. Because of this, she stepped up to the plate and gained her certification in bookkeeping. She began by helping her father with his business and later extended services to local and nonlocal business alike.
